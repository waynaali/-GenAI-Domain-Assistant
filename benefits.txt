Employee Benefits - Company Benefits Policy

Health Insurance:
All full-time employees are eligible for company-sponsored health insurance after 30 days of employment.
The company covers 70% of the premium, while employees pay the remaining 30%.

Retirement Plan:
Employees can enroll in the company retirement savings plan after 6 months of employment.
The company matches employee contributions up to 5% of their salary.

Learning and Development:
Employees receive an annual learning budget of $1000 for courses, certifications, and conferences.

Wellness Program:
The company provides a monthly wellness stipend of $50 that can be used for gym memberships, fitness classes, or health-related activities.
