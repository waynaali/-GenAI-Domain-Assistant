Employee Handbook - HR Policies  Vacation Policy: All full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation days accrue monthly and can be used after 90 days.  Remote Work Policy: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work requires manager approval.  Parental Leave: 12 weeks paid parental leave for primary caregivers. 6 weeks paid leave for secondary caregivers.
